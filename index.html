Description of a triangle.

A triangle is a plane figure with three straight sides and three angles.

Triangle types:

  1. Equilateral triangle
  2. Iscoceless triangle
  3. Scalene triangle

Equilateral triangle.

An Equilateral triangle is a triangle which three sides are equal.

Iscoceless triangle.

An Iscoceless triangle is a triangle wich two sides are equal

Scalene triangle.

A Scalene triangle is a triangle wich both sides are an equal.
